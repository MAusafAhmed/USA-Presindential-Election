About Us

The Electoral College in the U.S. Presidential Election is composed of 538 electors. Each U.S. state is allocated a varying number of electoral votes based on its total representation in Congress : 435 Representatives and 100 Senators plus 3 votes from the District of Columbia (Washington D.C., the U.S. capital)
